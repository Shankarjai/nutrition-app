Introduction

Ah, I see! Apples are a popular and nutritious[1] fruit that belong to the Malus genus. They come in a variety of types, including sweet, tart, and tart-sweet varieties, with common ones being Red Delicious, Granny Smith, Fuji, and Gala. Apples are rich in vitamins (especially Vitamin C), fiber, and antioxidants, making them a healthy addition to any diet. They're versatile in cooking and baking, commonly used in desserts like apple pie[2], crisps, and tarts, as well as in salads, juices, and sauces. Apples have been cultivated for thousands of years and are one of the most widely consumed fruits worldwide.
